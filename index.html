Tour Challenge — How It Works

Season Overview

Tour Challenge is a season-long PGA Tour pick 'em competition. Compete across 9 events — Signature Events, Majors, and FedEx Cup Playoffs — to crown a season champion.

Event Types & Multipliers

Signature Events (1x) — Quick Pick format. Choose 3 golfers from the field. Your best 2 of 3 finishing positions count toward your score.

Majors (3x) — Full Draft format. Pick 8 golfers across 5 tiers (1 + 2 + 2 + 2 + 1). Your best 4 of 8 count. Tiebreakers: closest to winning score, correct playoff prediction, correct Saturday leader.

FedEx Cup Playoffs (2x) — Same Full Draft format as Majors.

Season Points

After each event, your pool finish converts to season points, then multiplied by the event category:

1st — 10 pts
2nd — 7 pts
3rd — 5 pts
4th — 3 pts
5th — 2 pts
6th-10th — 1 pt

Example: Win a Major = 10 × 3x = 30 season points. Win a Signature = 10 × 1x = 10 season points.

Highest cumulative season points at the end of the year wins the Tour Challenge.

Tiebreakers (Majors & Playoffs)

If two or more players have the same raw score, ties are broken by:

1. Closest prediction of the winner's final score vs. par

2. Correct playoff prediction (Yes/No)

3. Correct Saturday moving day leader

Bonus Predictions

For each event, answer 3 bonus questions when you submit picks:

Pick the winner — +3 pts if correct

Winning score — +2 pts (closest wins)

Will the 54-hole leader win? — +1 pt if correct

Max +6 bonus points per event. These are added to your season total.

My Guy (Season-Long Pick)

Each member drafts one PGA Tour player as their "My Guy" for the season. Bonus points are earned whenever your guy performs well at any pool event:

Win — +5 pts · Top 5 — +3 pts · Top 10 — +1 pt
